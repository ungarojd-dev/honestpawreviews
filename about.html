About HonestPawReviews

Independent reviews of dog training programs and pet health products. No paid placements. Real testing.

Why We Started This

Every dog owner has been there. You search for help with a behavior problem, and you find page after page of vague advice, glowing reviews that all sound identical, and programs that promise everything. It is nearly impossible to tell what actually works.

HonestPawReviews was built to cut through that. We test programs ourselves, track real behavioral changes over time, and report what we find honestly. If something did not work, we say so. If something surprised us, we say that too.

How We Test

Each program we review is tested exclusively for a minimum of two weeks with at least one dog. We track specific behaviors before, during, and after the testing period so our observations are based on actual data rather than impressions.

  • Each program tested for a minimum of two weeks
  • Specific behaviors tracked and documented throughout
  • No program is reviewed based on marketing materials alone
  • Pricing and availability verified before every publish
  • Reviews updated when anything material changes

Our Editorial Principles

🔍

No Paid Placements

No product has ever paid to appear on this site. Rankings are determined entirely by testing results.

💬

Honest About Limitations

Every review includes what did not work and who the product is not right for. No product is perfect for every dog.

📅

Kept Current

Prices change, programs update, products get discontinued. We revisit every review regularly.

🐕

Real Dogs Only

Every program we review has been tested with real dogs in real home environments, not controlled lab conditions.
